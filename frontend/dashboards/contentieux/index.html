Dispute Resolution Framework (Articles 23-26)

Article 23: Dispute Submission

Citizens and businesses may submit disputes regarding tax assessments, penalties, or calculations within the statutory period.

Article 24: Commission de Révision

Disputes are submitted to the Commission de Révision for initial review. The Commission evaluates:

  • Validity of the claimed amount
  • Calculation accuracy
  • Supporting documentation
  • Applicable law and regulations

Article 25: Commission Review Process

The Commission may:

  • Accept the dispute claim (reduce tax)
  • Reject the claim (maintain original tax)
  • Partially accept (adjust tax by recommended amount)

Article 26: Final Decision & Appeal

The Contentieux Officer issues the final decision based on Commission recommendation:

  • Tax is adjusted accordingly
  • Penalty may be waived or reduced
  • If disputed, claimant has right to appeal to superior court
  • All decisions documented for audit trail
